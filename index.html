Ramp — Getting & Using Your Card

Once approved, here's how to expense your OYOD spend

✅
Only request a Ramp card once you have approval — or if your course is already on the discipline list and your lead has signed off.
Steps to get your Ramp card
Log in to Ramp and request the OYOD virtual card — it's specifically for professional development spend.
Your manager will receive a notification to approve the card — give them a heads up so it moves quickly.
Once the card is issued, use the virtual card number to register for your course or conference.
Attach your receipts in Ramp and add a memo with the course/conference name and how it relates to your work in Trust.
If your expense is declined or you're unsure which card to request, ping your lead or post in #trust-people-leads.
